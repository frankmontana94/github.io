Bill Gates

Who is Bill Gates?

William Henry "Bill" Gates III was born in Seattle, Washington on October 28th, 1955. He is best known for being the co-founder of Microsoft and being one of the wealthiest individuals in the world. As of January 2017, his net worth reached 88.5 billion dollars. Gates started his interest in computers and computer software as a teen in high school. After he graduated, he attended Harvard University, but Gates dropped out to further pursue his passion in software development. In the Bill Gates note link, Gates explains how the microprocessor increased a computer's potential. The microprocessor needed software, so Gates and a former Harvard collegue, Paul Allen, had the vision to create software for the microprocessors and start up their business, Micro-Soft.

How did Gate's work influence the world?

Bill Gates corporation successfully created a computer program called Disk Operating system, which was initially sold to IBM (Internation Bussiness Machines) in 1980 and IMB used that software for its first line of personal computers. It is explained in link, IBM and Microsoft, how this production of computers with Microsoft compatable hardware was copied by competitors and the competitors themselves turned to purchase Microsoft software as well.

This chain of events made Microsoft extremely successful and eventurally turned Bill Gates into a billionaire. He was so successful his competitor Apple CEO, Steve Jobs asked Gates to bail out his company from bankruptcy for 150 million dollars, so thanks to Gates people can continue to enjoy Apple products to this day. Gates indirectly contributed to society by the technological influence Microsoft Co. has had around the world, but he has also directly contributed to societies around the world, through the Bill and Melinda Gates foundation. The foundation has donated over 30 billion dollars in grants to health programs all around the world.
